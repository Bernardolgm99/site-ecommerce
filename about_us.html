ABOUT US

Welcome to Drink'O'City! The Drink'O'City was created with the initial purpose of a mere university work group but, at the moment, it is one of the largest companies nationally and is managed by our 3 founders, Beatriz, Bernardo and Eduardo. The purpose of Drink'O'City is to deliver high quality drinks everywhere at affordable prices.

At the moment we onyl deliver tea, coffee and vermouths but soon we will have more drinks, such as energy drinks, carbonated drinks and much more.
